Bootloader Versions
===================
* 0.0.1 .. 0.0.2
  - Early versions ported from the Tiva TM4C1294 Connected LaunchPad.
  - Not included in the Git repository.
* 0.0.3 - 28 Aug 2020
  - First functional version of the serial bootloader on the MDT-TP CM
    demonstrator MCU.
- 0.0.4 - 29 Jun 2021
  - Ensure that the LED red 1 is on when the boot loader is active.
* 0.0.5 - 22 Mar 2022
  - Explicitly marked that this bootloader is made for the MDT-TP CM
    demonstrator.
